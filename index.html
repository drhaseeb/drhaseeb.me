About

I am a dedicated and experienced medical professional, currently working as a General Practitioner at the Doctors Medical and Surgical Center in Shinkiari, Mansehra. This is a newly established clinic that aims to provide comprehensive and affordable healthcare services to the local community.

Prior to this, I have worked as a Medical Officer in Internal Medicine at the Shaukat Khanum Memorial Cancer Hospital and Research Centre Peshawar, a renowned tertiary care hospital that holds the prestigious Joint Commission International (JCI) accreditation, reflecting its commitment to the highest standards of healthcare. I have also gained valuable experience in various other disciplines at the Ayub Teaching Hospital in Abbottabad, another leading tertiary care hospital in the region.

Throughout my career, I have cultivated a diverse skill set across multiple disciplines, including Internal Medicine, Emergency Medicine, Critical Care, General Surgery, Pediatrics, Orthopedics, and Palliative Care. My expertise lies in delivering exceptional healthcare services, ensuring patient safety and satisfaction.

I possess a profound understanding of Electronic Medical Records (EMR) systems, which enables me to efficiently manage patient data and streamline clinical workflows. Additionally, I am proficient in Microsoft Word and Microsoft PowerPoint, along with various other IT-related software, which enable me to thrive and excel in the modern workplace.

My commitment to delivering quality healthcare is unwavering. I am constantly seeking new knowledge and experiences to further enhance my capabilities as a healthcare provider. My goal is to make a meaningful impact in the lives of my patients and contribute positively to the field of medicine.
