About

I am a dedicated and experienced medical professional, currently serving as a Medical Officer at the Shaukat Khanum Memorial Cancer Hospital and Research Centre in Peshawar. This institution is renowned for its state-of-the-art facilities and holds the prestigious Joint Commission International (JCI) accreditation, reflecting its commitment to the highest standards of healthcare.

Throughout my career, I have cultivated a diverse skill set across multiple disciplines, including Internal Medicine, Emergency Medicine, Critical Care, General Surgery, Pediatrics, and Orthopedics. My expertise lies in delivering exceptional healthcare services, ensuring patient safety and satisfaction.

I possess a profound understanding of Electronic Medical Records (EMR) systems, which enables me to efficiently manage patient data and streamline clinical workflows. Additionally, I am proficient in Microsoft Word and Microsoft PowerPoint, along with various other IT-related software. These technical skills enhance my ability to communicate effectively with colleagues and patients alike.

Beyond my professional responsibilities, I have a passion for web design, which allows me to explore my creative side and stay abreast of technological advancements. Traveling and hiking are among my favorite leisure activities, offering me opportunities to unwind and connect with nature.

My commitment to delivering quality healthcare is unwavering. I am constantly seeking new knowledge and experiences to further enhance my capabilities as a healthcare provider. My goal is to make a meaningful impact in the lives of my patients and contribute positively to the field of medicine.
